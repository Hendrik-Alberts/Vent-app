ARDS/Sepsis Ventilation Algorithm

    Note:
  • Call Critical Care MDc for pH < 7.29 or > 7.55
  • Max Set VR = 30
  • Initial PEEP Setup = 8 mmHg
  • Measure cuff pressures
    • Air: at referring, altitude, landing
    • Ground: referring

    Goal: pH 7.30 - 7.45 & PaO2 > 55 or SpOx > 88

    Use with Normal Oxygenation High PEEP Table

    COPD/ASTHMA Ventilation Algorithm

      Note:
  • Call Critical Care MDc for:
    • pH <= 7.20
    • PEEP > 10 mmHg
  • Max Set VR = 25
  • Initial PEEP Setup = 8 mmHg
  • Measure cuff pressures
    • Air: at referring, altitude, landing
    • Ground: referring

    For patients with S/S exacerbation

    Goals: pH target > 7.20

    PaO2 >= 55 or SpO2 = 88

    MV = Minute Ventilation

    VR = Ventilatory Rate

    VT = Tidal Volume
